SMS Consent Policy

This page explains how participants in the Secret Santa App give consent to receive text messages. The app is a small, private project run by Matthew Sable for coordinating Secret Santa gift exchanges among friends.

How Consent Is Collected

  • Participation is by invitation only. No unsolicited messages are sent.
  • Before being added to the event, each participant verbally confirms their consent to receive a single SMS message containing their Secret Santa assignment.
  • Messages are only sent to phone numbers that have been provided directly by consenting participants.

Message Details

Each participant receives one message such as:
“Hi Joe, you have Scott for Secret Santa!”

No marketing, promotional, or recurring messages are sent. Messages are sent only once per event, and only to participants who have opted in.

Opt-Out and Contact

Participants can opt out at any time by contacting the organizer directly before the message is sent. For questions, please email duke.sable@gmail.com.
