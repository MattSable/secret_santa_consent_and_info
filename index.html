Secret Santa App

The Secret Santa App is a small personal project created by Matthew Sable to help friends organize anonymous gift exchanges. The app randomly assigns each participant a gift recipient and sends out one simple text message with their match.

How It Works

  1. Participants verbally agree to take part in a Secret Santa event.
  2. Each participant provides their phone number directly to the organizer.
  3. The app sends one text message with their assigned recipient (e.g. “Hi Joe, you have Scott for Secret Santa!”).
  4. No marketing or recurring messages are sent — only one-time notifications for confirmed participants.
